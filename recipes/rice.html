Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is an Asian staple with endless variations. Enjoy this Chinese style fried rice alone or with a meat-based main dish.

Ingredients

  • White rice, calrose or jasmine
  • Water
  • Onions, chopped
  • Garlic, grated
  • Carrots, chopped
  • Broccoli, chopped
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Soy sauce
  • Peanut oil
  • Chili powder
  • MSG powder

Method

  1. Rinse rice thoroughly and cook in cool water. Allow rice to cool before cooking fried rice.
  2. Heat a wok on medium-high heat and add peanut oil. Wok is ready for cooking when the oil shimmers.
  3. Saut√© onions and garlic for a few minutes till onions are translucent and browning.
  4. Add remaining vegetables and spices. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant and steaming.
  5. Stir in the rice and liberally add soy sauce. Cook for 10 minutes to marry all the flavors.
  6. Mix in MSG powder until fully dissolved and remove rice from heat. Serve immediately.
