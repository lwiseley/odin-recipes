Maltesers and Ice Cream

Description

Maltesers are a perfect pairing for ice cream. Enjoy this simple but delicious dessert when you need a treat.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate ice cream
  • Maltesers
  • Chocolate syrup

Method

  1. Smash your maltesers into smaller pieces.
  2. Scoop ice cream into a bowl and top with smashed Maltesers and chocolate syrup.
  3. Enjoy immediately or share with a friend!
