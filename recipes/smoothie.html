Pineapple Smoothie

Description

Smoothies are an amaxing way to enjoy the goodness of fresh fruit in a convenient and delicious package. Enjoy for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • Pineapple chunks, fresh or soft frozen
  • Frozen yogurt
  • Pineapple juice
  • (Optional) Protein powder

Method

  1. Add pineapple and frozen yogurt to a blender jar. Top with juice.
  2. Blend thoroughly before lightly pulsing in protein powder to thicken texture and improve nutrition.
